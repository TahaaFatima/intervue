Our Story

InterVue, Job Board Application

A dynamic group of six passionate women from diverse tech backgrounds came together to address a common challenge faced by job seekers with disorganized interview preparation. As part of a Women in Tech initiative, they collaborated to create InterVue, a centralized web application designed to help users manage interview preparation, track applications, and increase their chances of success. Our web application, InterVue, is a centralized and user-friendly platform that empowers users to take control of their career journey. It helps them organize interview materials, manage deadlines, track job applications, and prepare with confidence in one place. By leveraging our unique skill sets in backend development, frontend design, business analysis, and quality assurance, we created InterVue not just as a tool, but as a companion for career growth. Our mission is to foster accessibility, confidence, and success in the job market.
